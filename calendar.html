Events & Calendar

Pack Schedule

Below is our regular schedule and upcoming events. For the most current information, please contact us!

Regular Meetings

📅 Pack Meetings

When: First Monday of each month at 6:00 PM

Where: Cupertino Community Hall or Lincoln Elementary School

All scouts and families welcome. Pack meetings include activities, announcements, and recognitions.

👥 Den Meetings

When: Twice monthly (days vary by den)

Where: Various locations throughout Cupertino

Each den meets on their own schedule. Contact us to find the den that fits your family's schedule.

🎉 Pack BBQ & Family Picnic

Location: Memorial Park, Cupertino

Join us for food, games, and fun! This is a great opportunity for interested families to meet current scouts and parents and learn more about joining Pack 415.

🏕️ Summer Camping Trip

Date: July 2026 (TBD)

A fun overnight camping adventure for our scouts to practice outdoor skills and build friendships.
