Welcome to Pack 415

We are an active Cub Scout Pack based in the Monta Vista area of Cupertino, serving youth for over 50 years. Our year-long program features outdoor events and physical activities designed to promote character development, leadership skills, and confidence in youth.

📋 Regular Meetings

Pack meetings are held on the first Monday of each month at 6pm at Cupertino Community Hall or Lincoln Elementary School.

👥 Den Groups

Dens (smaller groups) meet twice monthly on days chosen by families. Each den provides an intimate setting for scouts to learn and grow.

🏕️ Outdoor Activities

We organize one or two outings each month featuring camping, hiking, and other outdoor adventures to build skills and friendships.

🎉 Special Events

Throughout the year we host special events like our Pack BBQ and other fun gatherings for families and scouts.
