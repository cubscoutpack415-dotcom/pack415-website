Cupertino & CUSD elementary schools

Cub Scouting for Cupertino families.

Pack 415 is a parent-run Cub Scout pack for K-5 scouts across Cupertino and CUSD elementary schools, building character and community since 1954. We are officially chartered by the Rotary Club of Cupertino.
