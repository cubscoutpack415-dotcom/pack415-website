About Pack 415

Our History

Cub Scout Pack 415 has been serving the Cupertino community for over 50 years. We are proud of our tradition of developing young leaders and instilling values of character, citizenship, and outdoor skills in our scouts.

Our Mission

To prepare young people to make ethical and moral choices over their lifetimes by instilling values such as trustworthiness, loyalty, helpfulness, friendliness, courtesy, kindness, obedience, cheerfulness, thriftiness, bravery, cleanliness, and reverence.

Age Groups & Dens

Cub Scouts (K-5)

Our main program for kindergarten through 5th grade scouts. We focus on outdoor skills, team building, and character development through fun activities.

Den Structure

Scouts are organized into dens (typically 4-8 scouts per den) led by experienced den leaders. Each den meets twice monthly at times that work for their families.

Pack Activities

All dens come together for monthly pack meetings and special events like camping trips, BBQs, and community service projects.
